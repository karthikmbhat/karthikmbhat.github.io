Design of Low Cost 500VA Standalone Solar Inverter


This work presents Stand-alone photovoltaic systems are used in rural areas to power various basic loads, typically less than 300 VA to 500 VA. These basic loads includefan, lamps, TV, Radio and mobile chargers. A conventional stand-alone photovoltaicsystem consists of three power electronic converters. They are maximum powerpoint tracker (MPPT), Battery charge controller and inverter. MPPT controller extracts maximum power from the PV panel. Charge controller facilitates the chargingand disharging of the battery as per the load demand and power availability. If the load power is more than the extracted power from PV, battery discharges to supply additional power, excess power is stored in the battery. The loads are fed through the inverter which converts DC power from PV or battery to AC power which is single phase 230Vac, 50Hz.
